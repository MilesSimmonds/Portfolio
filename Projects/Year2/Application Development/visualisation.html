Claims by Car Type

The above chart displays the amount of claims each car type has produced. From this, we can dictate the trends in claim frequency across different car types, enabling us to identify potential areas for risk mitigation or further investigation. Additionally, it helps in identifying any patterns or correlations between car types and claim frequencies, which can inform insurance pricing strategies and policy adjustments to better manage risk and optimize profitability.

Average Cost by Car Type

The above chart displays the average cost and claims each car type has produced. By examining which car types have higher claim frequencies and average costs, employees can identify vehicles that pose higher risks to the insurance company. This information can guide decisions on premium pricing and risk assessment.
